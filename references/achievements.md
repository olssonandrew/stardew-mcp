# Stardew Valley — Achievements

**Achievements** are displayed on the player menu, under the "Collections" tab, on the "Achievements" tab found at the bottom left, on every platform other than Xbox and PlayStation. If playing the game on Steam or PlayStation, additional achievements are available.

Achievements can unlock specific rewards within the game. Achievements for specific platforms, such as Steam, only need to be achieved once, but the associated rewards must be earned separately for each new game.

Unearned achievements appear as grayed-out on the player menu. Some achievements do not display on the player menu until completing other achievements.

Note that only in-game achievements are required for Perfection.

Most achievements unlock a unique hat for sale at the abandoned house in Cindersap Forest. In addition, the day after earning "Gofer" (Complete 10 Help Wanted requests), Mayor Lewis will send 500g in the mail. The day after earning "A Big Help" (Complete 40 Help Wanted requests), Mayor Lewis will send 2,500g in the mail. The day after earning "Master Angler" (Catch every fish), Willy will send a Stardrop in the mail.

## Notes

### Fishing Achievements

Catching Green Algae, White Algae, Seaweed, River Jelly, Sea Jelly, or Cave Jelly counts towards the 4 fishing achievements (Fisherman, Ol' Mariner, Master Angler, and Mother Catch). Catching Trash does not count towards these achievements. Catching the quest fish for the Extended Family Qi's quest are not required to obtain the Master Angler achievement.

### Polyculture

There are exactly 28 Crops that must be shipped to earn the Polyculture achievement ("Ship 15 of each crop"):

**Spring Crops**

- Cauliflower
- Coffee Bean
- Garlic
- Green Bean
- Kale
- Parsnip
- Potato
- Rhubarb
- Strawberry

**Summer Crops**

- Blueberry
- Corn
- Hops
- Hot Pepper
- Melon
- Radish
- Red Cabbage
- Starfruit
- Tomato
- Wheat

**Fall Crops**

- Amaranth
- Artichoke
- Beet
- Bok Choy
- Cranberries
- Eggplant
- Grape
- Pumpkin
- Yam

### Monoculture

To earn the Monoculture achievement ("Ship 300 of one crop"), one of 33 Crops can be chosen -- any of the 28 crops listed above, or

- Ancient Fruit
- Blue Jazz
- Fairy Rose
- Summer Spangle
- Tulip

### Full Shipment

To earn the Full Shipment achievement ("Ship every item"), at least one of every item shown in the Shipping Collection tab of the player menu must be shipped.

### 'Help Wanted' Quests

The weekly quests on the Special Orders board and the weekly quests on Qi's Special Orders board count towards the Gofer ("Complete 10 'Help Wanted' requests") and A Big Help ("Complete 40 'Help Wanted' requests") achievements.

### Friendship Achievements

The level of friendship with the player's children affects friendship achievements ("A New Friend", "Best Friends", "The Beloved Farmer", "Cliques", "Networking", and "Popular").

### Crafting Achievements

The Wedding Ring does not count towards crafting achievements ("D.I.Y.", "Artisan", and "Craft Master"), even in Multiplayer games.

## Retroactive Achievements

An achievement may not unlock properly in rare cases (*e.g.,* because the game couldn't send the achievement to the underlying platform like Steam). Most missed achievements will be unlocked automatically when loading the save or sleeping in-game; if a specific achievement doesn't unlock, see the table below.

On PlayStation and Xbox consoles only:

:   The game isn't allowed to resend trophies/achievements automatically. It will instead resend those when a relevant action is performed (see table below).
:   **Bug:** an achievement may unlock without the matching trophy getting unlocked (*e.g.* because you were offline). If you slept in-game after that happens, it's not possible to unlock the trophy again on that save. As of the upcoming Stardew Valley 1.6.16: this is fixed in the next update.
